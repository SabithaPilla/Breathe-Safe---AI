BreatheSafe AI harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to predict health risks associated with pollution exposure. Focusing on harmful PM2.5 particles, which contribute to 1.67 million deaths annually, our platform analyzes air quality data to highlight risks in urban areas exceeding WHO limits. We empower individuals and organizations to take proactive measures, ensuring a healthier and safer environment for all.
